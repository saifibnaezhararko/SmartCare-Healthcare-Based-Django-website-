Recommended Vaccination Schedule

  • Birth: Hepatitis B (1st dose), BCG
  • 2 months: DTaP, IPV, Hib, PCV13, Rotavirus
  • 4 months: DTaP, IPV, Hib, PCV13, Rotavirus
  • 6 months: DTaP, IPV, Hib, PCV13, Hepatitis B (3rd dose)
  • 9 months: Measles, Mumps, Rubella (MMR - 1st dose)

  • 12-15 months: Varicella, Hepatitis A (1st dose)
  • 15-18 months: DTaP (4th dose), MMR (2nd dose)
  • 4-6 years: DTaP, IPV, MMR, Varicella
  • 11-12 years: Tdap, HPV, Meningococcal

  • Annual: Influenza vaccine
  • Every 10 years: Tdap or Td booster
  • Age 50+: Shingles vaccine (Shingrix)
  • Age 65+: Pneumococcal vaccine (PCV13, PPSV23)
  • As recommended: COVID-19, HPV (up to age 26)
