First Aid for Burns

Immediate Steps:
  1. Cool the burn with running water (10-20 minutes)
  2. Remove jewelry and tight clothing
  3. Cover with clean, dry cloth
  4. Do NOT apply ice directly
  5. Do NOT apply ointments or butter
  6. Seek medical help immediately
Burn Severity Levels
Minor

First-degree burns, redness, pain

Moderate

Second-degree burns, blisters

Severe

Third-degree burns, deep tissue damage

Critical

Fourth-degree burns, life-threatening

Prevention Tips
  • Keep hot liquids away from children
  • Use back burners when cooking
  • Test bath water temperature
  • Keep fire extinguishers handy
  • Install smoke detectors
  • Store chemicals safely
