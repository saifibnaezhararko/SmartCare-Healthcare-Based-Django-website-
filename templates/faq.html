Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

General Questions

Our hospital is open 24/7 for emergency services. Regular OPD hours are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. Some departments have specific timings - please contact us for detailed information about specific services.

Yes, we strongly recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting times. You can book appointments online through our website, by phone, or by visiting our reception desk. Walk-in patients are also welcome, but may experience longer wait times.

SmartCare Hospital is centrally located in the heart of the city. Please visit our Contact Us page for detailed address, map, and directions. We have ample parking facilities available for patients and visitors.

Appointments & Billing

You can book an appointment through our website by selecting your preferred doctor and available time slot. Alternatively, call our helpline or visit our reception desk. You'll need to provide basic information like name, contact details, and reason for visit.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance. You can do this through our website, by calling us, or by visiting in person. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), and bank transfers. For major procedures, we also offer payment plans. Insurance claims are processed for patients with valid health insurance.

Yes, we work with most major health insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card and necessary documents during your visit. Our billing department will assist you with the insurance claim process.

Medical Services

We offer comprehensive healthcare services including general consultation, specialist care, diagnostic services (lab tests, X-ray, MRI, CT scan), surgery, emergency care, maternity services, pediatric care, dental services, physiotherapy, and more. Visit our Services page for complete details.

Yes, our emergency department operates 24/7 with experienced emergency physicians, fully equipped ambulances, and advanced life support systems. Call our emergency hotline for immediate assistance.

Most routine lab tests are ready within 24 hours. Some tests may take 2-3 days, while specialized tests might take longer. Imaging reports (X-ray, CT, MRI) are typically available within 24-48 hours. Emergency tests are prioritized for faster results.

Patient Care

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, previous medical records, and any relevant test results or imaging reports. This helps our doctors provide better care.

Yes, we encourage patients to seek second opinions for major diagnoses or treatment plans. Our doctors will provide you with copies of your medical records and test results upon request.

Yes, visitors are welcome during designated visiting hours. Please check with the nursing station for specific timings. We may limit the number of visitors per patient to ensure a peaceful healing environment.
